Ranking

COMSATS University Islamabad is one of the highest ranking universities of pakistan.Its lies second according to HEC ranking among Pakistani universities and lies between 601-800 among worldwide universities.

Faculty

The faculties of COMSATS University Islamabad are very good especialy the faculty of Computer Science. Teachers are very cooperative and hardworking. They train the students theoraticaly as well as practicaly.

Location

COMSATS University Islamabad has very good location especialy in the sense as it is located in the capital city of Pakistan where students which come from villages find many oppertunities to avail which they donot have there in their local universities.
